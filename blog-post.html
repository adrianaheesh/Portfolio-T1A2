5 Reasons Why My Cats are Beautiful Angels

It's no secret that cats are the best. They eat the spiders in your house, purr on your lap, and don't require additional maintanence like bathing or walking.

I personally have 2 cats. They are both domestic breeds, whatever that means. Anyway here's a list (not exhaustive) of why they're awesome.

  1. Mila has an adorable moustache
  2. Sebastian, gets FOMO when I close the door behind me. He's a stage 5 clinger and I'm here for it.
  3. Mila is chunky. It's very cute.
  4. Sebastian loves to cuddle.
  5. They both like to watch David Attenborough documentaries with me.

In conclusion, you should have a pet cat.
